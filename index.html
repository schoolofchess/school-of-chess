About the Founder

Coach T. Muthukumar is a FIDE-rated player and certified National Instructor with over a decade of experience training competitive chess players across various levels.

Since 2016, he has been actively associated with Chess Gurukul, one of India's leading chess training academies, contributing to the development of numerous talented young players through structured and disciplined coaching methods.

His students have achieved success at the State, National, and International levels, earning medals and significant rating progress through consistent training and tournament preparation.

Several students have progressed from unrated to ~2000 FIDE rating within approximately three years through systematic training.

Our Methodology

Elite Training Philosophy

Every session at School of Chess follows a proven, structured methodology designed to build real chess strength — not just rating points.

01

Calculation Training

Systematic tactical exercises to improve accuracy, depth of analysis, and the ability to calculate complex variations under time pressure.

02

Visualization Improvement

Board blindfold exercises and pattern recognition drills to build the ability to see several moves ahead without moving pieces.

03

Positional Understanding

Learning to evaluate imbalances — pawn structures, piece activity, king safety — and convert positional advantages cleanly.

04

Guess the Move Practice

Studying grandmaster games move by move to build intuition, improve pattern recognition, and understand master-level thinking.

05

Tournament Discipline

Preparing mentally and physically for competitive play — clock management, psychological resilience, and consistent decision-making.

06

Practical Decision-Making

Training students to make good decisions in complex positions — balancing calculation, intuition, and risk assessment effectively.

07

Endgame Studies

Mastering fundamental and complex endgames — the phase where technical precision separates good players from great ones.

08

Structured Thinking Process

Building a reliable, repeatable thought process for every move — reducing blunders and improving consistency across all game phases.
